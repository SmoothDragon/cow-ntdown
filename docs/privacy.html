Cow-ntdown Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Cow-ntdown is a one-minute countdown timer app published as a side project of Cows in Space.

Data we collect

Cow-ntdown does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app runs entirely on your device. There is no account system, no analytics SDK, no advertising network, and no remote server for app usage.

Permissions

The app may use device audio to play bundled sound effects while the timer runs. It does not access the microphone, contacts, location, photos, or other personal data.

Children

Cow-ntdown does not knowingly collect data from children. Because it collects no personal data at all, it is suitable for general audiences.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
